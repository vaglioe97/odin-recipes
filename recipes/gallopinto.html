Gallo Pinto Recipe

Recipe Description

Gallo pinto is a Costa Rican dish of rice and beans.

Its name translates to "spotted rooster" because the cooked beans give the white rice a speckled appearance.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons canola oil.
  • 1 onion, chopped.
  • 1 red bell pepper , chopped.
  • 2 cloves garlic , minced.
  • 2 cups cooked black beans , in 3/4 cup reserved cooking liquid.
  • ¼ cup Salsa Lizano.
  • 3 cups cooked rice , preferably, day-old and refrigerated.
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh cilantro.

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add onion, pepper, and salt.
  3. Lightly cook until the onion is translucent
  4. Add the beans with broth and toss with the chopped vegetables and oil.
  5. Add the rice (cooked) to the skillet.
  6. Toss until well combined with the beans and spices.
  7. Add the cilantro and lightly toss.
  8. Heat thoroughly and add the Salsa Lizano.
  9. Mix again and then serve preferably with fried eggs, warm corn tortillas, and a hot cup of Costa Rican coffee or agua dulce.
